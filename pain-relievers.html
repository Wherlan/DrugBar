List of available pain reliever medications

Available Pain Relievers

Ibuprofen

Indication: Mild to moderate pain relief.

Name: Ibuprofen

Indication: Used for mild to moderate pain relief, such as headaches, toothaches, and menstrual cramps.

Dosage Forms: Tablets, liquid suspension, topical gel.

Manufacturer: XYZ Pharmaceuticals

Manufacturing Date: 2023-02-10

NAFDAC Number: 04-1234

Registration Expiry: 2025-06-30

Side Effects: Nausea, stomach pain, dizziness.

Warnings: Avoid in patients with ulcers or kidney disease. Take with food to reduce stomach irritation.

Storage Conditions: Store in a cool, dry place.

Other Information: Commonly available over-the-counter for pain relief.

Acetaminophen

Indication: Effective for reducing fever and relieving pain.

Name: Acetaminophen

Indication: Effective for reducing fever and relieving pain from various conditions.

Dosage Forms: Tablets, capsules, liquid suspension.

Manufacturer: ABC Pharmaceuticals

Manufacturing Date: 2022-08-15

NAFDAC Number: 05-5678

Registration Expiry: 2024-12-15

Side Effects: Nausea, skin rash, liver toxicity (overdose).

Warnings: Overuse may cause liver damage. Do not combine with other acetaminophen-containing products.

Storage Conditions: Store in a cool, dry place.

Other Information: Often used in combination with other medications like codeine.

Aspirin

Indication: Pain relief and anti-inflammatory.

Name: Aspirin

Indication: Used for pain relief and to reduce inflammation; also acts as a blood thinner.

Dosage Forms: Tablets, enteric-coated tablets, chewable tablets.

Manufacturer: DEF Pharmaceuticals

Manufacturing Date: 2023-05-20

NAFDAC Number: 06-9101

Registration Expiry: 2026-03-10

Side Effects: Stomach irritation, bleeding, ulcers.

Warnings: Not suitable for individuals with bleeding disorders or children under 12 (risk of Reye's syndrome).

Storage Conditions: Store in a cool, dry place.

Other Information: Commonly used to prevent heart attacks in low doses.
