Available Cardiovascular Medications

Amlodipine

Indication: Used to treat high blood pressure and angina.

Lisinopril

Indication: Helps manage high blood pressure and heart failure.

Atorvastatin

Indication: Reduces cholesterol levels and risk of heart disease.

Metoprolol

Indication: Used to treat high blood pressure and heart rhythm disorders.

Clopidogrel

Indication: Antiplatelet agent to prevent blood clots.

Losartan

Indication: Used to treat high blood pressure and protect kidney function.

Digoxin

Indication: Used to treat heart failure and irregular heartbeats.

Furosemide

Indication: Used to treat edema and high blood pressure.

Simvastatin

Indication: Lowers cholesterol and triglyceride levels.

Warfarin

Indication: Anticoagulant used to prevent blood clots.
